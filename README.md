\# Calmify AI 🎵🧠



\## Overview



Calmify AI is an AI-powered Mental Health and Music Recommendation System designed to assess the mental well-being of students and provide personalized recommendations based on their stress levels, lifestyle habits, and emotional state.



The project combines Machine Learning, Data Analysis, and Web Technologies to help users gain insights into their mental health and discover suitable wellness and music recommendations.



\---



\## Problem Statement



Mental health challenges among students have increased significantly due to academic pressure, social expectations, excessive screen time, and lifestyle changes.



Calmify AI aims to:



\* Assess mental well-being through questionnaires and behavioral indicators.

\* Predict stress and burnout levels using machine learning models.

\* Provide personalized recommendations for wellness and mood improvement.

\* Recommend music and healthy habits that may positively influence mental well-being.
